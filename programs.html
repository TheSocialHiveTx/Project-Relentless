Programs & Mission

What We Do

Six pillars of healing, purpose, and community for those who served.

Our programs aren't programs — they're missions. Each one is designed around what veterans already know: that brotherhood, purpose, challenge, and shared experience are the most powerful medicine there is. We've built six pillars, each addressing a different dimension of the veteran experience.

Program 01

Humvee Convoys & Off-Road

There's something that happens when a veteran climbs back behind the wheel of a Humvee, convoy in formation, dust flying, radio crackling — the noise in the mind quiets. Operation Relentless convoy events are purpose-built for this transformation.

Our off-road runs restore the adrenaline, the focus, the camaraderie, and the unmistakable sense of mission that civilian life often strips away. Whether you're a seasoned convoy commander or a first-timer, you'll find your people on the trail.

✓ Mission-style convoy formations
✓ All experience levels welcome
✓ Adventure-driven mental wellness
✓ Open to veterans, service members & families
See Upcoming Runs
Program 02

Peer Support

Nobody understands a veteran like another veteran. Operation Relentless peer support is built on the proven truth that connection — real, honest, judgment-free connection — can save lives.

We facilitate veteran-to-veteran conversations, small-group gatherings, and one-on-one check-ins that reach people where the clinical systems often can't. If you're struggling — or you know someone who is — this community will show up.

✓ Veteran-led support circles
✓ Confidential and non-clinical
✓ Bridge to professional resources when needed

Program 03

Suicide-Prevention Awareness

Veteran suicide is a crisis that demands a community response — not whispers. Operation Relentless confronts it head-on through bold awareness campaigns, community presence, and programming that directly combats isolation.

We partner with local organizations and use our platform — every convoy, every event, every gathering — to normalize conversations about mental health and remind veterans that their life has mission-critical value.

Crisis now? Dial 988, Press 1  ·  Text 838255  ·  Available 24/7
Talk to Someone
Program 04

Community Outreach

Healing doesn't happen in isolation. Operation Relentless reaches into the Deer Park community and beyond — partnering with churches, local businesses, civic organizations, and military families to weave a safety net that catches veterans before they fall.

From benefit events that put tangible help in the hands of families in need, to awareness tables at community gatherings, we go where veterans are — and we stay.

✓ Benefit events for veteran families in crisis
✓ Local church & civic partnerships
✓ Resource navigation and referrals

Program 05

Next-Gen Mentorship

The values that made you a good soldier, sailor, airman, or Marine — discipline, accountability, selfless service — are the same values the next generation desperately needs. Operation Relentless connects veteran mentors with youth and young adults to pass the torch.

This program creates meaning for mentors and momentum for mentees. Both walk away stronger.

Become a Mentor
Program 06

Faith & Fellowship

For many veterans, faith is the anchor that held through deployment, loss, and the long road home. Operation Relentless honors that foundation by creating space for faith-based gatherings, prayer, and spiritual community that complements our broader programming.

This isn't one-size-fits-all religion — it's brotherhood and sisterhood around shared values, with space for each person's individual faith journey. All are welcome.
