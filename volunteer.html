Where You're Needed

Volunteer Opportunities

Whether you have a day or a skill set, we have a role. Every position is mission-critical.

Event Crew

Setup, logistics, registration, tear-down, and general operations for BBQs, tributes, and community gatherings. If you've ever run a mess hall, you can run an event tent.

Setup & Teardown Registration Logistics On-Call Day-Of

Convoy Support

Vehicle operators, safety monitors, trail scouts, and recovery support. If you know off-road, 4WD, or vehicle mechanics — this is your lane. Experience with Humvees or similar a plus.

Vehicle Ops Trail Safety Recovery Support Navigation

Community Outreach

Resource tables, awareness tabling, church partnerships, and neighborhood outreach. Help us reach the veterans who don't know we're here yet. People skills and a mission heart are all you need.

Tabling Resource Navigation Church Partnerships Canvassing

Skills & Trades

Mechanics, welders, electricians, builders, cooks, photographers, videographers, graphic designers, IT professionals — if you have a skill, we have a use for it. Bring what you've got.

Mechanics Media & Photo Design & Tech Culinary Construction
