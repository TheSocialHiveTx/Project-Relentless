Our Story

About Operation Relentless

Faith · Brotherhood · Purpose · Adventure-Driven Healing

Who We Are

Born from Service.
Built for Those Who Served.

Operation Relentless was founded in Deer Park, TX with a simple, unshakeable conviction: the duty to serve doesn't end when the uniform comes off. The men and women who gave years — sometimes entire careers, sometimes their lives — to protect this country deserve a community that refuses to let them disappear into silence.

Built around the rugged spirit of off-road adventure, faith, and brotherly connection, we create real, tangible experiences that rebuild the bonds veterans lost in transition. A Humvee convoy through rough terrain. A benefit dinner that puts groceries on a family's table. A late-night conversation with someone who has walked the same road.

We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Every dollar given, every hour volunteered, every member enrolled goes directly toward keeping veterans connected, supported, and alive.

What Drives Us

Our Four Core Values

🛡️

Service

We lead by example, giving back to the community that shaped us with the same dedication we brought to the mission overseas.

⚖️

Integrity

We do what we say. Transparent operations, honest stewardship of donor resources, and accountability to every veteran we serve.

❤️

Compassion

We meet people where they are — without judgment. Every veteran's journey is different, and every story deserves to be heard.

🤝

Unity

Branch, background, belief — none of it divides us here. We stand together as one community bound by shared sacrifice and mutual respect.

The Mission

Operation Relentless is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to reconnecting veterans, active-duty service members, veterans' spouses, Gold Star families, and their communities through camaraderie, purpose, faith, brotherhood, sisterhood, and adventure-driven healing. Guided by values of service, integrity, compassion, and unity, we combat isolation, strengthen mental wellness, prevent veteran suicide, and build a lasting support network.
