Quick Answers

Common Questions

Who can participate in OR events?

All veterans, active-duty service members, military spouses, Gold Star families, and community supporters. Nobody turned away — all are welcome at our events and programs.

How can I submit photos from an event?

Use the contact form on this page, select "Photo Submission," and include details about the event. We review and add approved photos to our gallery.

Is Operation Relentless a 501(c)(3)?

Yes. Operation Relentless, Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Your donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. EIN: [EIN Placeholder]

How do I apply for peer support?

Use this contact form, select "Peer Support / I Need Help," and our team will connect with you. For immediate crisis support, dial 988, Press 1.
